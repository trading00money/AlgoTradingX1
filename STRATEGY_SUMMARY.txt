  SECTION 2 — ACTIVE STRATEGY: v5 EMA20 PULLBACK

NAME      : Pullback Entry Strategy v5
INSTRUMENT: XAUUSD (MT5 symbol: GOLD)
TIMEFRAME : H1 (hourly bars, signal on bar close)
FILE      : core/signal_engine.py
            method: AISignalEngine.generate_signals_for_backtest()
            lines : 777 – 971

CONCEPT
  Enters on pullbacks to EMA20 within confirmed trending regimes.
  Unlike crossover strategies (v1–v4), entries occur AT support/
  resistance rather than after it breaks, giving tighter risk and
  better statistical entry prices.

--- ENTRY CONDITIONS (LONG) ---

  Pullback trigger (price action):
    - Bar low touches EMA20 zone:
        low <= EMA20 + 0.5 × ATR  AND  low >= EMA20 – 1.0 × ATR
    - Close above EMA20 (support held, bullish rejection)

  Ehlers state confirmation (all three required):
    - MAMA > FAMA                 (MESA adaptive trend bullish)
    - ITrend > ITrend_trigger     (DSP trendline bullish)
    - EBSW > –0.5                 (not in cycle downturn)

  Regime gate (all required):
    - H1 EMA200 bull:  close > EMA200  AND  EMA200_slope(20) > 0
    - D1 EMA50  bull:  daily close > D1 EMA50
    - ADX > 15                    (minimum trend strength)
    - DI+ > DI– + 1               (directional buffer)
    - Session: 07:00–20:00 UTC    (London + New York only)

--- ENTRY CONDITIONS (SHORT) ---
  Mirror of long conditions with bear equivalents:
    - High touches EMA20 zone from below
    - Close below EMA20 (resistance held)
    - MAMA < FAMA, ITrend < trigger, EBSW < +0.5
    - H1 EMA200 bear, D1 EMA50 bear, ADX > 15, DI– > DI+ + 1
    - Session: 07:00–20:00 UTC

--- COOLDOWN ---
  5-bar global cooldown between consecutive signals.
  Prevents back-to-back entries in choppy zones.

--- EXIT LOGIC ---
  Four independent exit mechanisms (managed by LiveTrader
  in manage_open_position(), checked every H1 bar close):

  1. Take Profit (MT5 managed)
     Full TP order at entry ± 5.0 × ATR × 3.0 = 15.0 × ATR
     This is a ceiling; regime exit captures the 3–4R sweet spot.

  2. Stop Loss (MT5 managed)
     Hard stop at entry ± 3.0 × ATR(14).

  3. Partial Exit at 3R  (script managed)
     When unrealized P&L >= 3 × initial risk distance:
       - Close 50% of position at market
       - Move SL to entry price (breakeven)
     Converts stalled winners to locked-in gains.

  4. Time Stop at 30 bars  (script managed)
     If still underwater (unrealized R < 0) after 30 H1 bars:
       - Close 100% of position at market.
     Cuts slow bleeders before the full SL is hit.

  5. Regime Exit  (script managed)
     If signal flips direction while in a trade:
       - Long position + SELL signal  → close immediately
       - Short position + BUY signal  → close immediately
     Uses fast Ehlers exit regime (majority-vote: 2 of 3 Ehlers
     indicators flip) which responds 5–15 bars earlier than the
     slow EMA200+ADX regime gate.

Source file: config/risk_config.yaml (flat keys section)

--- POSITION SIZING ---
  Method         : Fixed fractional
  Risk per trade : 0.5% equity  (paper phase, live script constant)
                   1.0% equity  (backtested value; post-scale-up target)
  Short scale    : 0.5×  (short trades use half normal size)
                   Rationale: shorts carry higher tail risk on gold;
                   asymmetric sizing preserves their diversification
                   benefit while limiting drag on equity curve.
  Lot formula    : lots = (equity × risk%) / (SL_distance × 100)
                   where 100 = oz per standard lot on MT5 GOLD

--- STOP LOSS ---
  Method         : ATR-based
  ATR period     : 14
  Multiplier     : 3.0 × ATR  (walk-forward selected, 7/16 folds)
  Direction      : long  SL = entry – (3.0 × ATR)
                   short SL = entry + (3.0 × ATR)

  Vol-adaptive SL (enabled):
    Above 70th ATR percentile → scale multiplier down linearly
    Floor multiplier           : 1.5×  (extreme volatility)
    Hard SL cap                : $100/oz maximum distance
    Rationale: prevents outsized dollar losses during gold volatility
    spikes (2021–2022 rate hike period).

  Trailing stop  : DISABLED
    Tested on 5yr MT5 data; hurt PF at all settings tested.

  Breakeven stop : DISABLED
    Tested: cuts avg_win more than avg_loss (PF 1.90 → 1.83).
    Replaced by partial exit at 3R + move SL to BE.

--- TAKE PROFIT ---
  Method         : RR ratio
  RR ratio       : 5.0  (TP = entry ± 5.0 × risk distance)
  This is a ceiling only. The regime exit mechanism captures
  the 3–4R sweet spot in practice. Lowering RR to 3.0 was tested
  and reduced PF from 1.96 to 1.68 because regime exits lost value
  without the 5R ceiling to anchor the best winners.

--- PARTIAL EXIT ---
  Enabled        : Yes
  Trigger        : 3.0R unrealized profit
  Close amount   : 50% of remaining position
  After trigger  : SL moved to entry price (breakeven)
  Rationale      : At 3R (60% of the way to 5R TP), trade has
                   enough confirmation to lock gains. 2R was too
                   early and cannibalized best winners.

--- TIME STOP ---
  Enabled        : Yes
  Bars threshold : 30 H1 bars (~1.25 trading days)
  Condition      : Close if still underwater (unrealized R < 0)
  Rationale      : Converts full SL losses into smaller time stop
                   losses on stagnating trades.
